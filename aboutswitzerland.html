Understanding Poverty in Switzerland

Despite Switzerland's wealth, poverty is rising because many people have a poor income and cannot fully take part in normal daily life. More than 700,000 people in Switzerland are affected by poverty, and many others live only slightly above the poverty line.

Poverty is not only about money it affects opportunities, confidence, and participation in society. Caritas Switzerland works to support people socially, financially, and emotionally.

Switzerland's 10 Goals to Reduce Poverty by 2030

  • Everyone has enough for basic needs
  • People at risk get help early
  • Social support stays strong
  • Jobs pay fairly
  • Children grow up without poverty
  • Affordable housing for all
  • Help is available in tough situations
  • Equal chances across the country
  • Migrants are supported to integrate
  • A strong and fair economy
